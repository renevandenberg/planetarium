Eisinga's Digital Planetarium

Explanatory notes

This planetarium is a simple simulation of the planetarium in Franeker. The celestial bodies are from center to outer ring: the Sun, Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter and Saturn.

The rotational speeds are as Eisinga approximates them, calibrated at the planet's positions in 1781. As those are not sufficiently precise to appromiximate nowaday's planet positions, it is possible to choose De Lalande's rotational speeds, that remain to be sufficiently accurate.
